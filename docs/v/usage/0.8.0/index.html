新增稜鏡街機面板

現在多了一款「稜鏡街機（Prism Arcade）」面板可以選了！它有著深紫黑色的背景，中央有一圈緩慢旋轉的彩虹光暈，搭配隨機漂移的幾何碎片（三角形與菱形）和閃爍的彩色光點粒子。卡片邊框採用了全息漸層效果，進度條更是全光譜的彩虹色並帶有掃光動畫，看起來非常酷炫。就像是在你的電腦桌面上擺了一台充滿復古霓虹燈效的經典大型機台。

Original changelog
**New "Prism Arcade" panel**: deep purple-black background, Canvas conic rainbow halo rotating slowly, geometric prism shards (triangles/diamonds) drifting randomly, coloured light particles flickering, cards with holographic gradient borders (CSS background-clip technique), full-spectrum rainbow progress bars with sweep animation.

新增黑洞視界面板，動畫背景更清晰、版面更服貼

現在多了一款「黑洞視界（Black Hole）」面板可以選了！它有著純黑的宇宙背景、120 顆閃爍的星星，中央是一個有著藍紫光暈的事件視界和旋轉的漸層吸積盤，搭配琥珀色的玻璃卡片，科幻感十足。同時，我們把水族箱、稜鏡街機與黑洞視界這三款動畫面板的卡片背景調得更透明，讓背後的動畫能更清楚地透出來；我們也修復了這些面板底部會莫名其妙留白的問題，現在內容會完美撐滿整個視窗高度了。就像是幫家裡的窗戶換了更透光的玻璃，而且原本裝歪、底部漏風的窗框也重新打上矽利康封滿了。

Original changelog
**New "Black Hole" panel**: pure-black space background, Canvas 2D star field (120 stars with twinkling), rotating accretion disk (orange-yellow-white gradient ellipse, Doppler brighter-left/darker-right), photon ring, event horizon with blue-purple glow, orange particles orbiting the ellipse, amber glass cards.
**Fix extra space at bottom of three panels**: added `flex: 1` to `.projects-card` in Aquarium, Prism Arcade, and Black Hole so content fills the full panel height.
**Reduce card opacity in three animated panels**: card background opacity lowered from 0.5–0.75 to 0.14–0.28 in Aquarium, Prism Arcade, and Black Hole so the background animations show through more.

New Prism Arcade panel added

You can now choose the new "Prism Arcade" panel! It features a deep purple-black background with a slowly rotating rainbow halo in the center, accompanied by randomly drifting geometric shards (triangles and diamonds) and flickering colored light particles. The cards sport holographic gradient borders, and the progress bars boast a full-spectrum rainbow sweep animation, making the whole thing look incredibly cool. It's like placing a classic arcade cabinet glowing with retro neon lights right on your computer desktop.

Original changelog
**New "Prism Arcade" panel**: deep purple-black background, Canvas conic rainbow halo rotating slowly, geometric prism shards (triangles/diamonds) drifting randomly, coloured light particles flickering, cards with holographic gradient borders (CSS background-clip technique), full-spectrum rainbow progress bars with sweep animation.

Black Hole panel added, and animated backgrounds are now clearer and properly fitted

You can now choose the new "Black Hole" panel! It features a pure-black space background with 120 twinkling stars, centered around an event horizon with a blue-purple glow and a rotating gradient accretion disk, paired with amber glass cards for a deeply sci-fi feel. Simultaneously, we've reduced the background opacity of the cards in the Aquarium, Prism Arcade, and Black Hole animated panels, allowing the animations behind them to show through much more clearly. We also fixed an issue where these panels had inexplicable empty space at the bottom, so their content now perfectly stretches to fill the full window height. It's like replacing your home's windows with clearer glass, and finally caulking up the crooked frames that were leaving a drafty gap at the bottom.

프리즘 아케이드 패널 추가

이제 '프리즘 아케이드(Prism Arcade)' 패널을 고를 수 있다! 짙은 자주색이 섞인 검은색 배경에 한가운데서 천천히 도는 무지갯빛 후광, 무작위로 떠다니는 기하학적 파편(삼각형과 마름모)과 깜빡이는 알록달록한 빛 입자들이 어우러져 있다. 카드 테두리는 홀로그램 그라데이션 효과를 입혔고, 진행 표시줄은 빛이 훑고 지나가는 애니메이션이 달린 모든 스펙트럼의 무지개색이라 무척 멋져 보인다. 레트로 네온 조명으로 가득한 클래식 오락기를 컴퓨터 바탕화면에 가져다 놓은 것과 같다.
